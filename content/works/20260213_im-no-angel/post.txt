Description:

Get your hands on this rock solid keycap set: DSA Granite, designed by Matt3o. This colorway stands strong on its own, but also provides a beautiful foundation for further customization with its timeless mix of light gray and white.

This keycap set comes with low-profile, durable PBT, dye-sublimated keycaps to create a keycap set as long-lasting as Half Dome and as free-standing as the peaks of Patagonia. Novelty keys allow you to add a few flecks of color and style, while staying true to the classic look. Modifiers include text modifiers, icon modifiers, the bonus pack, secondary legends, and more.

Here's what one reviewer said about the set:

"I had wanted the Granite keycap set for soooo long! I couldn't find it anywhere for the longest time as it was sold out everywhere! Finally PMK got them back in stock and I bought it up right away. The quality is great! I inspected every single keycap when they arrived and they are flawless!! They feel durable and high quality . . . You can't go wrong with this set, if you like the colorway then you'll love it. No quality control issues at all, they're perfect!"

In the words of Matt3o, “There are dozens of incredibly colorful and well designed keycap sets around, but nothing’s more elegant than a simple gray layout.” We couldn’t agree more. Give yourself the gift of this classic keycap set today.

Product Details:

This sublimated keycap set comes in GDE and GKK; the F and J keys are deep dish in the Common set.

RGB Set Colors: Blue: BCT, Red: RBH, Green: VAZ, Orange: OBB, Yellow: YR

Note: Sublimated Sets are printed on-demand and won't ship "same day" regardless of the shipping method chosen.
